About the Great Barrier Reef

The reef is a complex ecosystem that supports a diverse range of marine species, including fish, coral, turtles, and dolphins.
It's also a popular tourist destination for snorkeling, diving, and sailing.


Fact: Did you know that the Great Barrier Reef is visible from space?

Threats to the Great Barrier Reef

The reef faces several threats, including climate change, pollution, and crown-of-thorns starfish outbreaks. Efforts are underway to protect the reef and ensure its survival for future generations.

Fact:Climate change is the biggest threat to the Great Barrier Reef, causing coral bleaching and death.

Conservation Efforts

Various organizations and governments are working to protect the reef through initiatives such as marine protected areas, research, and education.
