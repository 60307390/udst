    • Frequently Asked Questions

    Q.  Why support for older and not-so-popular games?

    We have noticed that there is still a close-knit community for many of these "unpopular" games, and we have decided to serve their community by producing servers to play with friends.

    Q.  What all games can I host with your service?

    We have a wide variety of games including Minecraft, Rust, ARK, CS2, and much more! Check our our full game list on our products page.

    Q.  How much does it cost to host a server?

    Our pricing varies depending on the game and server specifications. Prices start as low as $5/month, with flexible plans to suit your needs.

    Q.  Can I customize my server settings?

    Yes! All our servers come with full access to configuration files and a user-friendly control panel, allowing you to customize settings, mods, and plugins as you like.

    Q.  Is there support for mods and plugins?

    Absolutely! For games like Minecraft, ARK, and others, we support mods and plugins from a variety of trusted sources like CurseForge, FTB, etc. For Source games (TF2, Garry's Mod, and so on), we support Steam Workshop plugins as well as custom modifications.

    Q.  What is your refund policy?

    We offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with our service, you can request a full refund within the first week of your purchase.

    Q.  Are my servers online 24/7?

    Yes, if you choose to. All our servers are hosted on reliable, high-performance infrastructure, ensuring they are online 24/7 with minimal downtime. If you wish, you can choose to stop the server for a period of time and resume according to your needs. Note that 24/7 hostings may have additional costs!
